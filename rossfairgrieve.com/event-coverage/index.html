Event Filming & Photography

“Events” covers an incredibly broad spectrum of occasions and event photography and videography is an equally broad field. Maybe you are arranging a social occasion and want photographs of the guests. Perhaps your company is arranging a promotional event and you need video coverage and photography to publicise it online or in print. Maybe your sports team has a big game on that you want filmed and photographed for prosperity (and the history books!). Whatever the nature of the event, my versatile and discreet style of photography and video production is sure to get you the results you need with the absolute minimum of hassle.

To discuss filming or photography for your next event, don’t hesitate to get in touch via the contact page or drop me an email at info@rossfairgrieve.com.
