I’m Back!

Hi team. Those of you who have been following me for a while will know that rossfairgrieve.com had been going for a year and a half or so, before a kind-hearted hacker recently tried to host a phishing scam on my site and then deleted the whole thing! Those who haven’t been following me for a while; hi, welcome and sorry the shelves of this site are looking a bit bare at the moment. Anyway, I told you I’d be back!

As well as keeping you updated with the varied and sometimes strange work that I’ve been doing, this blog did have several other facets including the popular “Photo School” section; a free resource designed to teach those new to photography and videography how to get started. Unfortunately, that content is no more but I’ll be rebuilding it as time goes on and will make sure that “RossFairgrieve.com 2” is even better than the original.

On the plus side, that hacker has taught me a lesson in internet security…and anger management. They’ve also given me the kick up the arse that I needed to re-jig the site to just how I want it. Over the coming days, weeks and months, I’ll be adding a lot of new features to the site including an interesting new twist on “Photo School” so be sure to follow me on Facebook and Twitter to keep up with developments as they happen.

I’ll leave it there and keep this short for now but “I’ll be back” soon [You see what I did there? “I’ll be”… Oh never mind].
